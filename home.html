About Our Company

Indo Thai Global is a leading manufacturer and supplier of innovative solar drying solutions and food processing equipment based in Ludhiana, Punjab. Since 2005, our systems have been designed to provide efficient, hygienic drying for agricultural and industrial applications.

We focus on sustainability, energy efficiency and long-term durability. Our equipment is trusted by farmers, food processing industries and entrepreneurs looking to reduce post-harvest loss and increase productivity.
